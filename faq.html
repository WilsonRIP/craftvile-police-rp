Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CraftVile Police RP

Getting Started

How do I join the server?

To join our server:

  1. Open Garry's Mod
  2. Click on 'Find Servers'
  3. Search for "CraftVile Police RP"
  4. Click Connect
  5. Make sure you have Counter-Strike: Source mounted

What are the server rules?

Our main rules include:

  • No RDM (Random Death Match)
  • No breaking character/RP
  • Respect other players
  • Follow police procedures
  • No exploits or hacks

View our complete rules on the community page.

Roles and Departments

How do I become a police officer?

To become a police officer:

  1. Reach Level 5 on the server
  2. Submit an application through the Community page
  3. Attend the training session
  4. Pass the final evaluation

Applications are reviewed within 48 hours.

What departments are available?

Available departments include:

  • Patrol Division
  • SWAT Team
  • Detective Bureau
  • K-9 Unit
  • Highway Patrol

Each department has specific requirements and training.

Technical Support

What addons do I need?

Required addons:

  • Counter-Strike: Source content
  • CraftVile Police Pack (auto-downloads)
  • DarkRP (auto-downloads)

All other addons will download automatically when you join.

How do I report bugs or issues?

To report issues:

  1. Join our Discord server
  2. Go to the #bug-reports channel
  3. Use the provided template
  4. Include screenshots if possible

Our staff team will investigate and respond promptly.

Still have questions?

Join our Discord server or contact our support team for additional help.
