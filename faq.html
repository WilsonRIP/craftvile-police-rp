Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CraftVile Police RP

General Questions

What is CraftVile Police RP?

+

CraftVile Police RP is a serious roleplay server in Garry's Mod where players can experience realistic police work and law enforcement scenarios in a multiplayer environment.

How do I join the server?

+

To join our server:

  1. Own Garry's Mod on Steam
  2. Subscribe to our required addons
  3. Join our Discord community
  4. Connect to the server through the IP address provided in Discord

What are the minimum requirements?

+
  • Age: 16+ for regular players, 18+ for staff positions
  • Stable internet connection
  • Working microphone
  • Discord account
  • Basic understanding of roleplay concepts

Roleplay Questions

What roleplay styles are allowed?

+

We focus on serious, realistic police roleplay. This includes:

  • Traffic stops
  • Criminal investigations
  • Emergency response
  • Department procedures
  • Community policing

How do I become a police officer?

+

To become a police officer:

  1. Submit a police application through our forms page
  2. Complete an interview with senior staff
  3. Pass the training academy
  4. Complete field training with experienced officers

Technical Questions

What addons do I need?

+

Required addons are available through our Steam Workshop collection. The main ones include:

  • DarkRP
  • Police Job Modifications
  • Custom Vehicle Pack
  • Server-specific content pack

How do I report technical issues?

+

Technical issues can be reported through:

  • Our Discord support channel
  • In-game admin call system
  • Support ticket system on our website
